Cheesecake

This cheesecake recipe is our best recipe for cheesecake with a rich and creamy texture and cherry topping for an indulgent dessert that is also easy to make! It is the perfect dessert to serve at your next party.

Ingredients

  • 1 ¾ cups Honey Maid Graham Cracker Crumbs
  • ⅓ cup butter, melted
  • 1 ¼ cups sugar, divided
  • 3 (8 ounce) packages Philadelphia Cream Cheese, softened
  • 1 cup Breakstone's or Knudsen Sour Cream
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 (21 ounce) can cherry pie filling

Steps

  1. Step 1

    2. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (180 degrees C).

  2. Step 2

    3. Mix graham crumbs, butter, and 1/4 cup sugar together in a large bowl.

  3. Step 3

    4. Press crumbs onto bottom of 9-inch springform pan.

  4. Step 4

    5. Beat cream cheese and remaining sugar in large bowl with mixer until blended. Add sour cream and vanilla; mix well.

  5. Step 5

    6. Add eggs, 1 at a time, beating on low speed after each addition just until blended.

  6. Step 6

    7. Pour mixture over crust.

  7. Step 7

    8. Bake in the preheated oven until the center is almost set, about 1 hour to 1 hour 10 minutes. Run knife around rim of pan to loosen cake; cool before removing rim. Refrigerate cheesecake 4 hours.

  8. Step 8

    9. Top with pie filling before serving.
